The Nike Zoom Freak 2, Giannis Antetokounmpo’s second signature sneaker, dropped just in time for the NBA’s Orlando Bubble. The Freak 2 is marketed as an all-position shoe, featuring Zoom Air in the forefoot and soft responsive cushioning, as well as a molded TPU piece that provides the stability and control necessary to Eurostep all the way to the basket. Giannis’ parents’ names, Charles and Veronica, are featured on the sole, his brothers name are listed on the bottom and “Greek Freak” has been inserted on the heels.
